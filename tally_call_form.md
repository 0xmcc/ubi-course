# Tally Call Form

Use this as the source of truth for the Tally form linked from the "Schedule a call" CTA.

Form URL: https://tally.so/r/81jp5o

## Form Title

Tell me what you want to build

## Form Intro

Answer a few questions so I can understand your app idea, where you’re stuck, and whether the 8-week program is the right fit.

## Start With These Questions

These are the questions to launch with first. They keep the form short enough for warm leads while still giving enough context for a useful call.

### 1. What’s your name?

Type: Short answer

Required: Yes

### 2. What’s your email?

Type: Email

Required: Yes

### 3. What are you trying to build?

Type: Long answer

Required: Yes

Helper text: Describe the app, tool, automation, or software idea in plain English.

### 4. What have you done so far?

Type: Multiple choice

Required: Yes

Options:

- It’s just an idea
- I’ve written notes / specs
- I have mockups or designs
- I’ve tried building with AI tools
- I have a rough prototype
- I already have something live

### 5. Where are you getting stuck?

Type: Checkboxes

Required: Yes

Options:

- I don’t know where to start
- The idea feels too big
- I don’t know how to break it into buildable steps
- I tried AI tools and got stuck
- I don’t trust what AI gives me
- I need help debugging
- I need help deploying
- I need help deciding what features matter
- Other

### 6. Are you looking to actively work on this over the next 8 weeks?

Type: Multiple choice

Required: Yes

Options:

- Yes, I’m ready to build now
- Maybe, I’m exploring
- Not yet, I’m just curious

## Add These If Call Quality Is Too Low

These are useful qualification questions, but they add friction. Add them later if too many people are booking without enough urgency or fit.

### What best describes you?

Type: Multiple choice

Required: No

Options:

- Working professional
- Student
- Founder / solo entrepreneur
- Creator
- Other

### Why do you want to build this now?

Type: Long answer

Required: No

Purpose: Reveals urgency.

### If we both decide it’s a fit, are you open to investing in a high-support paid program?

Type: Multiple choice

Required: No

Options:

- Yes
- Maybe, depends on fit
- Not right now
