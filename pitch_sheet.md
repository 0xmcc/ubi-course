- I have an idea but no idea where to start  
- I feel like I need a technical cofounder  
- I open Cursor or Claude and feel overwhelmed

**Desired**: Independent builder who can go from idea → live app quickly using AI, no reliance on engineers, can debug, design, and ship repeated. Building apps feels routine, not intimidating. Can execute on any idea on demand.

- I’ve actually put something real in users’ hands  
- I have early users giving feedback and validating the idea  
- I feel like a builder, not just a person with ideas  
- I know how to go from idea → working product repeatedly
